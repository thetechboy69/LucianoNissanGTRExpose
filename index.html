How to stop content farms?

It's extremely quick and simple to do.

-Report them for Spam
(Report Account -> Posting Inappropriate Content -> Deceptive behavior and spam -> Spam, they are spamming the same type of vids over and over again to trick the algorithm).

-Share it to others with the hashtag #StopContentFarms.
People need to know how to make them stop so TikTok (or other platforms) ban the content farms.

-If you are a content creator and an account farms content using your creation, use your platform's DMCA takedown form. Ok, the slime videos are satisfying but the real creators are getting their content stolen for profit.

How to stop Supporting them?

Here's a simple list of actions to stop supporting them.

-Unfollow them (why would you follow them in the first place)

-Don't watch their vids, it'll give them more watch time so more money.

-Block them. Their vids won't be recommended to you so no way of accidentally making his wallet grow more.

-Tell other people about this. He isn't the only one. We need to stop these content farms on TikTok and leave only genuine creators who make good content and not just farming a CapCut Template.
